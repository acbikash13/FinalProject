About us

We are students studying computer science at NKU. This website is a project for our class Full Stack Application development. We did it under professor Nicholas Caprusso. Explore this page to learn about the game we created and have fun playing it.

How to play the Game?

  • Choose how many cards you want to play with.
  • Click the "Generate Card" button to create your card(s).
  • The game will begin and the caller will start calling out numbers.
  • Mark the numbers on your card as they are called out.
  • The first player to mark all the numbers on their card(s) yells out "Bingo!" to win the game.

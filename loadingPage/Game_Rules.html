Game Rules

Here are the rules for playing Bingo:

  • Each player gets an empty e-Bingo card.
  • Each player has 3 options at the top: Pause, Fill, and Done.
  • Each player starts by clicking on the "fill" button to fill their card with numbers.
  • Once everyone is ready, each player calls out one number and everyone cut out that number from their card.
  • Whoever makes the first win, clicks on Done.

    The rules for winning include:

  • You can win either by cancelling out a row, column or diagonal.
  • A player must cancel out 5 rows, columns, or diagonals to claim their BINGO.
  • Each row, column, or diagonal cancelled out would stand as one of the letters from "Bingo".

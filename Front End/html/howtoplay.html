How to Play

  1. Choose how many cards you want to play with.
  2. Click the "Generate Card" button to create your card(s).
  3. The game will begin and the caller will start calling out numbers.
  4. Mark the numbers on your card as they are called out.
  5. The first player to mark all the numbers on their card(s) yells out "Bingo!" to win the game.

It's that simple! Have fun playing Bingo Game.
